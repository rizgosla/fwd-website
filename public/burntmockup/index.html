The burnt story

A nickname
that stuck.

In college, Chef Paul Cao fried his spaghetti to crisp the bottom. His roommates called it burnt spaghetti. The name caught on. The food truck followed in 2010. Today the truck is a kitchen on Irvine Center Drive — same crispy edges, same scratch-made sauces, same chef.
